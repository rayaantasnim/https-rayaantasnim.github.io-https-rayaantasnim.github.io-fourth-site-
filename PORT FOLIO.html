Each coin and note tells a story of a nation’s history, culture, and identity. Holding them feels like owning fragments of distant lands. The thrill of discovering a new currency, studying its symbols, and preserving its legacy turns every addition into a mini treasure hunt. This hobby isn’t just about accumulation; it’s my passport to global appreciation.

    Learning Interesting Science: In my leisure time, I dive into 'Biggan Chinta,' a science monthly magazine that fuels my curiosity. Whether exploring cosmic wonders or Earth’s biodiversity, this magazine turns every page into an adventure.
